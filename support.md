# Hvala — Support

**Hvala** is an app for learning Serbian: travel a 3D map through 35 themed
stops and master real-world vocabulary one lesson at a time.

## Contact us

Have a question, found a bug, or need help with a purchase? We're happy to help.

We usually reply within 2 business days.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, go to **Settings**, and tap **Restore Purchases**. Make sure you
are signed in with the same Apple Account you used for the original purchase.

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple Account, not inside the app.
On your device open **Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions**, select **Hvala**,
and you can change or cancel your plan there.

### Is my progress saved?
Yes. Your learning progress is stored locally on your device and is kept between
sessions automatically.

### Which content is free?
The first 5 stops are completely free. Hvala Premium unlocks all 35 stops.
